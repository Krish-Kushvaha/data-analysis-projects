## 📁 Projects

### 🛒 1. Superstore Sales Analysis

**Dataset:** Kaggle Superstore Dataset (9,994 rows)
**Tools Used:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib

### 🔍 Objective

To analyze retail sales data and identify trends, profitability drivers, and areas of improvement for business decision-making.

### 📌 Key Findings

* **Technology** is the top-performing category in terms of sales
* Certain products generate **high sales but low profit** due to heavy discounts
* A subset of products consistently contributes to **losses**
* Seasonal patterns are visible in monthly sales trends

---

### 📈 Core Analysis Performed

* **Monthly Sales Trend Analysis**
  Identified seasonality and peak sales periods

* **Profit vs Sales Comparison**
  Highlighted the gap between revenue generation and actual profitability

* **Discount Impact on Profit**
  Revealed how excessive discounting negatively affects margins

* **Loss-Making Product Identification**
  Pinpointed underperforming products for potential removal or strategy revision
